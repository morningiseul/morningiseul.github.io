# Synopsis

“Sichuan’s Number One!”   
“The Peerless Tang Clan!”

Praised as the Divine Physician of Immortal Fate, as though the heavens themselves had finally answered the prayers of the mortal realm.   
The foremost prodigy of the Medicine Hall, direct disciple of the Poison Sovereign, future of the Sichuan Tang Clan, and hope of the Central Plains…

His only goals in life were to bring glory to his family name and push the boundaries of medicine.   
But what happens when he gets kidnapped by the beautiful leader of the Demonic Cult and suddenly ordered to share his bed?

“...I, um, would prefer our first time to take place in your Venerable One's bed. Perhaps, we could choose an auspicious date and…”   
“So you wish to hold a wedding ceremony with me.”   
“...Pardon me? Oh, that is—”

This was treatment.   
Nothing more, nothing less. Just treatment.   
Both what the Heavenly Demon desired and what Somin had to provide.   
This was simply just a cure for the Blood Rampage Affliction.

“Do not keep me waiting too long.”

How in the world was he supposed to resist the sweet seduction of a peerless beauty who also bore the deadliest epithet in the world?

A bittersweet tale of saving the martial world! Follow Tang Somin, a righteous physician of the Orthodox Faction determined to rid the world of the Blood Rampage Affliction, and Cheon Ilho, the Demonic Cult Leader who intends to seduce the doctor and claim him for himself!
